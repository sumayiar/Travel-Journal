About Me

I write my dream trips by hand before I book them.

I handwrote all these travel destinations in a notebook I got at a networking conference. Every page is a map of where I want to go next, and this whole dream travel journal is a work in progress. Right now, I am planning to hike on spring break, and this weekend I am going on a hike too. I also want to try a Moroccan hammam in Morocco, and I want to see the cherry blossoms in Kyoto, Japan. I have cherry blossom curtains in my bedroom and listened to "Kyoto" by Phoebe Bridgers a lot in freshman year of high school. I also want to see the Maldives because I am Muslim and they feel very Muslim-friendly.
